Knight's tour problem

A tutorial on how to solve the well-known problem designed for secondary school students

Project Description

Welcome to our course page on solving the well-known Knight’s Tour Problem in C. We have designed the materials for secondary school students aged 15-18 with no prior experience in programming. We cover the C basics in the Video section before teaching you how to solve the Knight’s Tour Problem by hand and by the backtracking algorithm. You are also encouraged to find more efficient algorithms as an extension activity in our last video tutorial. We have exercise questions after every video to make sure that you understand the content. In the Challenge section, we provide some additional exercises to hone your programming skills. You can also experiment with our chessboards under the Game section to see whether you can find a solution. Have fun!
